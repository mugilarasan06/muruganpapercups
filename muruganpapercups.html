About Us

Established in 2012, Murugan Paper Cups is a leading manufacturer of premium-quality, biodegradable paper cups in India. Our commitment to sustainability drives every aspect of our production, from sourcing eco-friendly raw materials to employing green manufacturing practices.

We are aligned with several United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly:
SDG 13 – Climate action through eco-friendly processes.
SDG 14 – Preventing marine pollution by avoiding single-use plastics.

Our Products

We manufacture a wide range of biodegradable paper cups suitable for both hot and cold beverages. Our cups are food-grade certified, customizable, and 100% compostable.
